What is EventStorming?

EventStorming is a rapid, collaborative workshop format for exploring complex business domains. By bringing together business experts and technical team members, EventStorming reveals how your business actually worksâ€”not how you think it works.

Using colored sticky notes on a long wall, participants model business processes as sequences of domain events, uncovering insights, identifying pain points, and discovering opportunities for improvement.

Delivery Formats

In-Person

2-day intensive format with physical wall space and sticky notes for high-energy collaborative exploration

Best for: Co-located teams, greenfield exploration, team building

Virtual

4-6 sessions of 2-3 hours each using Miro collaborative whiteboard and Zoom for distributed teams

Best for: Remote teams, complex domains, flexible scheduling

Hybrid

Virtual facilitation with team co-located or distributed, using Miro to include all participants

Best for: Teams with some remote members
